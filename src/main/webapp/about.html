For Students, By Students

StudyU, a web app developed by college students in the United States, provides college students an easy way to meet others in their courses, ask questions, and share resources. Unlike many current applications, StudyU encourages the formation of small groups of students in the same course taught by the same professor. This fosters a close connection between members, regardless of whether their collaboration is purely virtual or supplemented with in-person meetings.
