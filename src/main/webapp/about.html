For Students, By Students

StudyU provides college and university students an easy way to meet others in their courses, ask questions, and share resources. Unlike many of the current applications, StudyU encourages the formation of small groups of students who are in the same course taught by the same professor in order to foster a more personal connection between group members, regardless of whether they choose to work purely virtually or supplements their in-person meetings with StudyU. Additionally, StudyU was created by a group of current college and university students from diverse areas of the United States.
