Case Two

Problem


Philip is in his calculus class, and after talking to his fellow classmates, has noticed many of them would like to be able to work with others on problems outside of class, but have not formed any official group. He would also like to be part of a study group.

Solution


What does he do? Philip logs onto StudyU, uses the "Create a Group" to create his calculus study group, and gets his fellow classmates to log on and join his group so they can work together on problems, arrange times to meet, and share helpful resources that will help them with their upcoming exam.
